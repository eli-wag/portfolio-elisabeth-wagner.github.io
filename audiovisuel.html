SECOND-HAND ADVERTISING

DATE : 2021

PROGRAM : DUT MMI - 2nd Year

TEAM : 5 students

REQUEST: Create an advertisement, a clip, or a short film on the theme of time passing.

SUMMARY: This short film is a 1-minute advertisement promoting second-hand items. We aim to highlight the benefits of recycling and the possibility of using the same clothing across different eras. To do this, we decided to showcase the multiple lives of a scarf. We were inspired by the CNP Assurance ad, where we follow the different stages of life through various time periods, all in one continuous shot. We decided to adopt this continuity concept by focusing on the accessory through different periods to emphasize its timeless nature. These periods are distinguished by clothing choices and image treatments.

WAFFLE RECIPE

DATE : 2020

PROGRAM : DUT MMI - 1st Year

Team : 1 student

REQUEST : In the context of lockdown, create an audiovisual project without using the IUT's equipment. The task was to make a video not exceeding 3 minutes, in stop-motion or pixilation.

SUMMARY: One of my favorite pastimes during the numerous lockdowns was cooking, so I chose to use stop-motion technique to showcase a waffle recipe, set to music from the animated film "Asterix and Cleopatra." I made this video using a mini tripod and my phone, on my parents' kitchen table. This choice of subject, setting, and music allowed me to play with and integrate the "homemade" aspect imposed by the limited equipment I had available.
